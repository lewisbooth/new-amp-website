Moorland Self Drive

Since 1986 Moorland Self Drive has grown into a strong, traditional family business. Until 2018, their marketing consisted entirely of word of mouth and the same 33-year-old logo. We had the opportunity to refresh their brand and create a custom website to showcase the business, helping the company to establish a modern presence as it is handed over to the next generation.

Custom Content Management

To avoid the complex interface of a traditional Wordpress or Magento system for managing vehicles, we designed and built a custom dashboard using our AMP Content Management System.

Managing vehicle listings is easy and streamlined due to the workflow being designed from the ground-up, saving the client valuable time and effort on a daily basis.

Speed & Optimisation

Even with a fully custom AMP content management system, the Moorland Self Drive website loads virtually instantly on all devices with a rare perfect 100 Google PageSpeed score. This leads to more conversions, improved SEO rankings and better customer satisfaction.

Brand Development

Throughout the process, we consulted the client through choosing the right business name, logo, colour scheme and marketing strategy.

The business is growing quickly with a new custom vehicle division that doesn't fit under the Self Drive name, so we created the MSD Custom Commercials sub-brand to accomodate.

Photography

Over the course of a single afternoon we photographed the Moorland Self Drive garage, services and the current fleet of vehicles. We created a selection of 40 professional images that are used across social media, advertising, signage and the website.
